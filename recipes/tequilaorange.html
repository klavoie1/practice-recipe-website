Tequila Orange Fizz

Description

An alcoholic drink made of tequila and oranges.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 1 fluid ounce freshly-squeezed orange juice
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces anejo tequila
  • 4 ounces sparkling mineral water (such as Topo ChicoÂ®)
  • 1 wedge orange
  • flaked salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a rocks glass with ice. Add orange juice and tequila and top with mineral water. Garnish with orange wedge. If desired, sprinkle a pinch of salt over top.
